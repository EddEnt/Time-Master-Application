Welcome to your Employee Panel

Clock

Here you can clock in and out to start or finish your day. It will require your employee code, so make sure you remember it.

Time Record

View your past work activity. See when you have clocked in and out for the day. You will also see your expected pay in real time.

Time Request

Request time off from here. Input both the start and end date, plus the reason for your request. You can view the status of your request, plus prior requests that you have submited.

Schedule

Download your current schedule for the week. Managers will upload the schedule on their end, and allow you to view it at your convenience.
